Explore Our Spanish Highlights

Barcelona

A colourful blend of beaches, architecture and food. Visit La Sagrada Família, stroll along La Rambla and unwind by the sea.

Madrid

Spain’s capital offers grand boulevards, world-class museums and buzzing nightlife around every corner.

Seville

Cobbled streets, orange trees and flamenco. An ideal base for discovering the charm of Andalucían life.

Costa del Sol

Long sandy beaches, family-friendly resorts and golf courses overlooking the sea make this coast a UK favourite.

Balearic Islands

Mallorca, Menorca and Ibiza each offer a different vibe, from quiet coves to chic marinas and lively bars.

Canary Islands

Year-round sunshine, volcanic landscapes and a huge choice of resorts on Tenerife, Gran Canaria, Lanzarote and Fuerteventura.
